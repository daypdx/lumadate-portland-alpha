Portland controlled alpha

Privacy

Last updated July 17, 2026.

Use a city or neighborhood only. Do not enter a street address or another person's private information.

What the alpha stores

LumaDate has no accounts and does not send planning answers to a LumaDate server. Plan preferences, saved plans, and demo reminder settings are stored in your browser on this device. The starting-area field is not retained; it is replaced with the Portland default before browser storage is written.

What the alpha does not do

It does not track GPS, sell location data, send messages, schedule notifications, process payments, or make reservations. It currently uses no product analytics or advertising trackers.

Third-party links and hosting

Map, review, reservation, and ticket links open third-party services governed by their own policies. This site is hosted by GitHub Pages, which may process ordinary service logs under GitHub's privacy practices.

Delete local data

Use Clear demo data inside LumaDate or clear this site's browser storage. Because there are no LumaDate accounts, there is no server-side profile to delete.
