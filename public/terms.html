Alpha terms

Last updated July 17, 2026.

This is a testing prototype. Do not rely on it as the sole source for safety, availability, travel time, accessibility, weather, pricing, or business hours.

Demonstration plans

Recommendations use demonstration data and heuristic estimates. Always confirm details directly with venues, maps, ticketing services, and reservation providers before leaving.

No transactions or emergency service

LumaDate does not book, pay, call, text, monitor a date, or contact emergency services. Safety-share and reminder tools create copyable text only. In an emergency, contact local emergency services or a trusted person directly.

Testing participation

By using the alpha, you agree to use general Portland areas rather than private addresses, avoid entering sensitive information about another person, and independently verify any plan before using it.
